## Overview

RegLens AI is an enterprise-grade financial intelligence platform that transforms lengthy annual reports into actionable business insights.

The platform automatically extracts financial statements, calculates key financial ratios, identifies risks, generates regulatory assessments, and provides an AI-powered Executive Copilot capable of answering natural language questions about uploaded reports.

Designed for financial analysts, auditors, investment professionals, compliance teams, and executive leadership.
